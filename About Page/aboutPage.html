About Us

Welcome to BINUS KANTIN, the online ordering system
designed specifically for BINUS college students! We
understand that your time is valuable, and waiting in long
lines for food can be frustrating and time-consuming.

That's why we created BINUS KANTIN - a convenient
solution that allows you to skip the lines and order your
food online.

With our extensive network of food vendors located
on campus, you can easily find your favorite foods
without having to wait in line. At BINUS KANTIN, our
goal is to make your food experience at BINUS as
efficient and hassle-free as possible.

Our platform provides a seamless online ordering
experience, allowing you to browse menus, place
orders, and pay directly through our website.

Made by Binus students, for
everyone.
